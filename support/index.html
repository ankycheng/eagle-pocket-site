Frequently Asked Questions

How do I open my Eagle library?

Tap the library name in the top-left corner of the home screen, then tap Add Library. You can choose a library folder from your device's local storage, iCloud Drive, or a network share (such as a NAS).

How do I connect Google Drive?

When adding a library, select Google Drive as the source. You will be prompted to sign in with your Google account and grant access. Eagle Pocket will then let you browse and select an Eagle library folder from your Drive.

My library isn't syncing. What should I do?

  1. Open Settings and check the Sync status.
  2. If the status shows an error, try tapping Sync Now… → Full Resync.
  3. For Google Drive libraries, make sure you are still signed in under Settings → Google Account.
  4. Ensure you have a stable internet connection.

How do I clear the cache?

Go to Settings → Storage → Clear Cache. This removes all locally cached images. They will be re-downloaded as needed when you browse your library.
